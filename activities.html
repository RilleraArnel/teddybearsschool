ACTIVITIES

COLORING

Children are engaged in fun and creative coloring activities! Filled with joy and excitement while bringing up valuable lessons along the way!


SING-ALONG

Singing is learning in melody! Singing brings out the best out of children while catering to their musical talents. Learning becomes easier once the vocal chords are in tune with the rhythm!


EDUCATIONAL FIELD TRIPS

Museums, Sea World, NASA and more destinations are sure to bring about exciting and new ideas from the children's blossoming minds. Wonders of nature and space makes learning with us an adventure!


PETTING

Children interaction with highly trained and domesticated animals makes them appreciate life more. Fostering relationship abilities through petting is a unique way of giving early perspective about how relationships work.


TALENT SHOWS

Talents aren't meant to be hidden but instead are meant to be shown for the world to see. Children will be able to showcase and hone skills and abilities they themselves never knew they had in a theatre-like platform that is deemed world-class.
